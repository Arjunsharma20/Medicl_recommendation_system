Building a Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App with Django and Machine Learning

In todayâ€™s world, access to accurate healthcare information is more important than ever. We created a symptom-based disease diagnosis web app using Django and a Decision Tree Classifier model. It helps users identify potential illnesses based on reported symptoms.

The Problem

People often experience symptoms and want quick answers. Differentiating between diseases with overlapping symptoms is challenging. Our goal was to provide a convenient, accurate solution.

The Solution

  1. Symptom Input: Users enter their symptoms through an easy interface.
  2. Machine Learning Model: A Support Vector Classifier predicts diseases based on input symptoms.
  3. Prediction: The app predicts the most likely disease instantly.
  4. Additional Information: Provides description, precautions, medications, diet, and workout tips.

Key Features

  • User-Friendly Interface: Easy and intuitive for users to input symptoms.
  • Accurate Predictions: Machine learning ensures precise disease predictions.
  • Comprehensive Information: Includes descriptions, precautions, medications, diet, and workouts.
  • Educational Content: Informative articles to empower users with healthcare knowledge.

Conclusion

This web app combines healthcare and technology to make healthcare more accessible and user-centric. Users can quickly gain insights into their health, make informed decisions, and improve overall well-being.
